Our Mission

  • Establish a permanent sacred space for the worship of Goddess Durga in Atlantic Canada.
  • As the Bengali Community, celebrate our rich cultural heritage through religious ceremonies and cultural activities.
  • Preserve and promote unity, devotion, and tradition within the Bengali community in Atlantic Canada.
  • Inaugurate the Maritimes Durgabari by 2026 for Sharodia Society's Golden Jubilee 50th Durga Puja celebration.
  • Create a vibrant hub where devotees can gather year-round and feel a sense of belonging.

Plan and Progress

Our mission is to establish Maritimes Durgabari for our community through a carefully structured approach. Below outlines the planned phases:

2024

Phase I: Securing Land and Zoning Permits (Ongoing)

Fall 2024 - Early 2025

Phase II: Designing and Constructing the Temple Structure

Early 2025 - Fall 2025

Phase III: Commencing Durgabari Operations

Early 2026

Durga Puja 2026

Each phase includes detailed financial estimates and is supported by a dedicated team working tirelessly to ensure the success of this endeavour.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I am enthusiastic about Maritimes Durgabari and would like to contribute. How can I donate?

A: Contributing to Maritimes Durgabari is simple. You can make a donation through Interac eTransfer to sharodiasociety@gmail.com. When making the eTransfer, please include the word "Durgabari" in the description box to ensure easy recognition of your contribution. For other contribution methods, feel free to contact our team mentioned at the Contact section.

Q: Can I claim tax relief on my donations to Maritimes Durgabari?

A: Yes, any contribution to Maritimes Durgabari or Sharodia Society is considered a charitable donation. You can easily claim a tax credit for the eligible amount using the tax receipt we provide at the beginning of the next year to all contributors.

Q: I am enthusiastic about Maritimes Durgabari but unable to contribute immediately. Can I contribute in the future?

Absolutely! Many of our supporters have pledged to contribute at a later date. You can pledge an amount you're comfortable donating in the future, and we'll be in touch. Click here to pledge today.

Q: I would prefer to make small periodic contributions. Is this possible?

A: Certainly! Many of our supporters choose to make monthly or periodic contributions. You can pledge to make smaller periodic contributions by clicking here, and we'll provide further details.

Q: I want to support Maritimes Durgabari in other ways. How can I do that?

A: We welcome everyone to join and support Maritimes Durgabari in various capacities. Whether it's participating in Puja rituals, offering technical support, assisting with food preparation, or contributing to cultural events, there's a role for everyone. Learn more about volunteering opportunities at here.
